The LW001-BG PRO is a wireless smart, ultra-low power consumption, indoor & outdoor (IP67) LoRaWAN
Tracker. Ideal for optimizing logistic processes, asset management, warehouse and inventory
scenarios, outdoor tracking, theft protection and much more.
